Services & Fees

Medical Record Review
Comprehensive psychiatric and clinical record analysis for attorneys and insurance professionals. Includes a written summary of findings, chronological timeline, and identification of clinical inconsistencies.
$325/hour · Flat-fee options available, expedited services available for an additional fee
Expert Witness Testimony
Deposition and trial testimony on matters involving psychiatric standards of care, medication management, diagnostic criteria, and clinician scope of practice. Clear, credible communication to judge and jury.
$325/hour · Retainer required, 4-hour minimum plus travel time/expenses for trial
Case Screening & Consultation
Pre-litigation case review to assess clinical merit, identify strengths and weaknesses, and advise on the involvement of additional experts. Efficient and cost-effective for early case strategy.
$325/hour · Initial consult available
Expert Report Preparation
Detailed written expert opinions addressing causation, standard of care, and damages. Reports are structured for use in deposition, mediation, or trial and are thoroughly supported by current medical literature.
